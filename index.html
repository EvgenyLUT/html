Tyrr Warriors have got their power from Sapyros — an ancient Giant who received the power of wind from Sayha before the outbreak of war between gods and Giants.
Tyrr Warriors follow the path of Destruction. They have become free mercenaries because of their ungovernable nature.

They are strong and wear heavy armor. They fight mostly in the front lines, striking terror into their enemies.
They can increase their battle characteristics using their inner energy, they're also well-versed in any weapon.
Furious battle-cry given to them makes Tyrr Warriors much stronger, they can also become invincible in critical situations.

  • Healer

Iss Enchanters succeed in protecting their allies. They have skills inherited from Sorcerer of Dawn Leister.
They use such skills to empower themselves and their parties like no one else.
They can use auras which are somewhat similar to those which Sigel Knights use (if there are two enchanters in a party, only one of them can use auras)
they also combine melodies and dancing to achieve the maximal effect.

Enchanters can help their party members change their appearance and flee from the battlefield, they can also turn their opponents into butterflies or piglets.
They are also good at dealing with any short-range weapon and can empower an attack or neutralize mobs in no time.

Yul Archers are great at hitting their targets from a long distance.
New crossbows help them stay at a longer distance from their potential opponents and shoot at incredible speed, and their magic quivers are always full of bolts and arrows.
Yul Archers’s attacks may stun the enemies. Yul Archers can also summon a serpent which doesn’t need any control and distracts the opponents.

Yul Archers move at incredible speed and can isolate mobs which makes tanks’ task a lot easier. They can also rouse hatred among the enemies and weaken them.
